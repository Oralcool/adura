Morning Prayer

PSALM 118:24

This is the day the Lord has made; let us rejoice and be glad in it.

Heavenly Father, as the sun rises, so does our gratitude for a new day. Thank you for the gift of life, for the peace that surpasses understanding, and for your guiding light.

We ask for your wisdom to navigate the challenges ahead and your strength to walk in your ways. May our hearts be filled with your love, our minds with your truth, and our actions with your grace. Amen.
